Conveying identity and trust in MAM Engineering's redesigned website

This project sought to transform an outdated website into a competitive platform that clearly conveys MAM Engineering's identity and services to existing and potential customers.

Industry: Steel Fabrication My Role: Sole UI/UX Designer Timeline: 2.5 weeks (Oct 2019)

Wireframes

Having defined the website's target users and the site map, it was time to create a visual structure that addresses user needs. Keeping in mind the business and user goals, I drafted a set of wireframes that demonstrate the company's experience, showcase social proof, and enable users to quickly place inquiries and find relevant information. We presented these wireframes to MAM Engineering to communicate clear design direction while acquiring quick and accurate feedback. After a couple of back-and-forth iterating placements and details of elements, the wireframes were approved. I proceeded to establish a consistent visual language for the new website, from which I created the hi-fidelity mockups.
