Increasing brand awareness with MRC Knox's new website

Materials Recovery Centre Knox is a waste management facility that provides essential disposal services, and sells recycled products. The goal of the web design is to create a platform that increases their online visibility and inform customers of their services and products.

Industry: Recycling My Role: Sole UI/UX Designer Timeline: 3 weeks (May 2020)

Wireframes

Following the approval of the new site map, the project team had discussions on the types of content that would go into each page. By analysing our competitors, we were able to determine current conventions and user expectations on similar sites. We also mapped out opportunities where Tilling could stand out from competitors. I started with creating initial wireframes of key pages. These were presented to the Project Manager and Tilling stakeholders. We went through an iterative process wherein wireframes were internally reviewed and tested before they were signed off.
